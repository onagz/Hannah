The Problem & Solution

The Problem

As a leader of young wives in my church and a mother myself, I kept hearing the same concern from women after childbirth: "I can't leave my baby, but I still want to work." Some women were forced to leave their jobs because it didn't allow flexibility, and their current income couldn't cover the cost of a nanny or creche. Others who had no job wanted to support their families but didn't know where to find safe, flexible remote work or free resources to build the right skills.

Unverified, scattered remote roles created friction. Paywalls blocked access to credible opportunities. There was no trusted hub that combined jobs with free skill resources.

‚úì

The Solution

Remote Carihers curates verified remote jobs and internships from female-friendly global companies, and pairs them with free resources and courses to build in-demand skills. Applications stay external, keeping the platform lightweight and aligned with real-world hiring.

Verified listings only, so women can trust what they see. A clear application flow without added friction. Free skill-building content to improve employability.

The Goal

I set out to build a focused MVP that would do the following:

  • Surface only verified remote jobs and internships through a simple, searchable job board.
  • Remove friction from job discovery with clear job details and external application flows.
  • Support women at different career stages with a free, curated resource hub for skill learning and building.

This goal directly shaped the MVP features: a trust-led Home experience, a lightweight Remote Jobs & Internships board, a Resources hub for upskilling, and minimal Settings for personalization.
